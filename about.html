TO KNOW MORE ABOUT US

Legalduniya.com was launched in 2016 with the simple mission of helping Entrepreneurs easily start and manage their business. From a small 2 member team in 2014 - while remaining bootstrapped and profitable, we grew to a 600+ strong team of employees. Today, we have the privilege of serving > 100,000 loyal, small business customers and over 1.6 million platform users. In 2021-22, we raised funding from institutional investors to further our mission globally and serve more customers. The journey continues…


CUSTOMER FIRST PHILOSOPHY

We are committed to building a fair, transparent, sustainable and performance driven company for the long-term. We believe the destiny of all businesses is decided purely by its customers. We believe when customers find value in the products and services offered by a business, a business would thrive. When customers do not find value in a business, a business would shut down. It is that simple. So, we place enormous importance on building value for our customers.
